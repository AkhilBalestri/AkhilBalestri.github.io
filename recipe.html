Italian Beef Recipe

Description

This Italian beef recipe is inspired by the classic Chicago-style preparation made famous by places like Johnnie’s Beef...

Ingredients

  • 3–4 lb beef top round or sirloin roast
  • 3 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Italian rolls
  • Giardiniera or roasted sweet peppers

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Combine all seasonings and rub them evenly over the beef roast.
  3. Place the roast in a baking dish and add beef broth, water, and bay leaves.
  4. Cover and cook for 2.5 to 3 hours.
  5. Remove and let cool.
  6. Slice the beef thin.
  7. Return sliced beef to warm broth and simmer.
  8. Serve on Italian rolls with sweet peppers or giardiniera.
